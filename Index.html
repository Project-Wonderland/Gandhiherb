What Are Herbal Products?

Herbal products are natural remedies derived from plants that have been used for thousands of years to support health and wellness. These products harness the therapeutic properties of herbs, roots, flowers, and other plant materials.

Unlike synthetic medications, herbal products work with your body's natural processes to promote healing and maintain balance.
